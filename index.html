Add New Bit

Consider Adding

  • Compression bit (1/4") — Clean both-side cuts in plywood
  • O-flute (1/8") — Better for plastics (acrylic, HDPE)
  • Ball nose (1/8") — 3D carving and sculpted surfaces
  • 90-degree V-bit — Wider/bolder lettering

Z-Probe Procedure
  1. Clip ground wire to the bit
  2. Place touchplate on TOP of workpiece
  3. Send: G38.2 Z-25 F100 P[thickness]
  4. Wait for contact — look for [PRB:...:1]
  5. Remove touchplate and ground wire
  6. Jog Z up slightly to verify
Safe Starting Settings (V1E Golden Rule)

When in doubt, start here:

  • Bit: 1/8" single-flute upcut (your NR 3)
  • Feed: 480 mm/min
  • Plunge: 180 mm/min
  • Depth/pass: 1-2mm
  • Kress dial: 2-3

Works on almost everything. Increase after you see good chips.
